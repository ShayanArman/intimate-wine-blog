## The Experience: What to Expect

Imagine relaxing in the comfort of your villa (Airbnb, VRBO, or agritourism), surrounded by friends and family, as our Certified Sommelier, Massimiliano (Massi) Nava, brings the Italian wine experience to life. Having grown up in the heart of Chianti, Massi’s deep-rooted passion for wine and people makes him the perfect guide to take you on this sensory adventure.

### What’s Covered in the Class

Our expertly designed session blends wine history, tasting techniques, and food pairing insights into one unforgettable experience. Over two hours, you’ll explore four topics that reveal the soul of Italian wine.

**1: Viticulture**

- Understand the foundations of winemaking, from grape training systems to the impact of soil types.
- Learn the differences between organic and conventional agricultural practices and how they shape wine quality.

**2: The Grape**

- Delve into grape characteristics and varieties that make Italian wines so distinct.
- Discover winemaking techniques for sparkling, white, orange, rosé, and red wines, as well as aging techniques that influence the final product.

**3: Wine Tasting**

- Develop your skills in wine examination through visual, olfactory, and taste analysis.
- Identify aromas, textures, and flavors that reveal the story of each bottle.

**4: Wine Pairing**

- Explore the Mercadini Method – a trusted Italian approach to pairing food and wine for the ultimate culinary harmony.
- Learn how to select wines that perfectly complement your meals, enhancing flavors and creating memorable dining experiences.
