People do not need one more dashboard for its own sake. They need help staying clear and organized in a communication channel that never stops.

That is the philosophy behind Zero Inbox. The problem is not a one-time cleanup. The problem is the inbox keeps rebuilding itself through everyday life.

## The idea behind the video

- Email is a continual problem, not a one-time project
- AI should make maintenance easier, not more reckless
- Permission matters because important email cannot be treated like disposable clutter

## Why Zero Inbox is different

Zero Inbox is designed to help people keep up with the ongoing need to clean and organize emails, while staying safe enough to use on a real inbox.
